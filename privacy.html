Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Ephemera is designed with privacy in mind. Your data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly use external services like AI features.

1. Information We Collect

Ephemera operates entirely within your web browser. Here's what is stored:

  • Local Data: All files, apps, and settings are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB and localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
  • Account Credentials: When you create a local account, your password is hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 and stored locally. We never see or store your password.
  • API Keys: If you configure an OpenRouter API key for AI features, it is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored locally. We never transmit or store this key on our servers.

2. Data Storage

All user data is stored in your browser using:

  • IndexedDB: For files, apps, and metadata
  • localStorage: For user profiles and session state
  • sessionStorage: For temporary session data

Data stored in these locations remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers unless you explicitly perform an action that requires it (such as using AI features).

3. Third-Party Services

Ephemera integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter API: Used for AI features. Requests are sent directly from your browser to OpenRouter. See OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.
  • CORS Proxies: Public CORS proxies (like allorigins.win) may be used to fetch external content. These proxies may log requests.
  • Sentry: We use Sentry for error tracking (optional). Error reports are anonymized and do not contain personal data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 before storage
  • Sensitive data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Session auto-locks after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you keep it in your browser. You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete All Data" feature in Settings → Data Management
  • Clearing your browser data for this site

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Export all your data via Settings → Data Management
  • Deletion: Delete all data via Settings → Data Management
  • Portability: Export data in JSON format
  • Opt-out: Disable error tracking

7. Browser Storage

Ephemera uses browser storage (localStorage, sessionStorage, IndexedDB) rather than traditional cookies. No data is sent to external servers except when you use AI features.

8. Children's Privacy

Ephemera is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through in-app notifications.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
